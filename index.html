About Our University

Discover our rich history, mission, and vision

Founded in 1985, Techville University has grown to become one of the leading educational institutions in the region. We have consistently ranked among the top universities for academic excellence, research output, and graduate employability.

Our campus spans over 100 acres with state-of-the-art facilities including modern laboratories, a comprehensive library, sports complex, and comfortable student accommodations.

Our History

Techville University was established in 1985 with just three departments and 200 students. Over the decades, we have expanded our academic offerings and infrastructure while maintaining our commitment to quality education.

Our Mission

To provide transformative education that empowers students to become innovative leaders, critical thinkers, and socially responsible global citizens.

Our Vision

To be a globally recognized center of academic excellence that drives innovation and positive change in society through education and research.

Admission Procedure

Our admission process is designed to identify talented and motivated students who will thrive in our academic community.

1

Application Submission

Complete the online application form with required documents.

2

Entrance Examination

Appear for the university entrance test (if applicable to your program).

3

Interview

Shortlisted candidates will be invited for a personal interview.

4

Admission Offer

Successful candidates will receive an admission offer letter.

5

Fee Payment & Enrollment

Complete the enrollment process by paying the admission fee.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Completion of 12 years of formal education
  • Minimum 60% marks in qualifying examination
  • Valid score in national/state entrance exams
  • English language proficiency (for international students)
  • Program-specific requirements as applicable
